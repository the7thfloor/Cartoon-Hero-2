Setsumi Sakura

About Setsumi

Setsumi is a terminally ill girl who resides on the 7th floor of an unnamed hospital in Japan. She enjoys memorizing information, particularly about cars and local geography.

Setsumi enjoying a walk

Characteristics

  1. She can identify the make and model of most cars just by glancing at them
  2. She is quiet and reserved, and it is very rare to hear her speak more than a few words at a time.
  3. She has been in and out of hospitals since she was in middle school.

Setsumi's friends
